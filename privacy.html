🌿 InnerVital – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

InnerVital stores all data locally on your device. We do not operate any servers and do not transmit your personal information over the internet.

Data stored locally includes:

  • Your selected wellness goals
  • Constitution assessment results and scores
  • Daily check-in ratings (sleep, energy, mood)
  • Task completion records
  • App language preference
  • Notification settings

2. Third-Party Services

InnerVital does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

3. Children's Privacy

InnerVital is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

4. Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply uninstall the app. All locally stored data will be removed from your device.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this page.

6. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at: gsf0425@163.com

🌿 InnerVital – 隐私政策

最后更新：2026年3月

1. 我们收集的数据

InnerVital 将所有数据存储在您的设备本地。我们不运营任何服务器，也不通过互联网传输您的个人信息。

本地存储的数据包括：

  • 您选择的养生目标
  • 体质测试结果及评分
  • 每日打卡评分（睡眠、精力、情绪）
  • 任务完成记录
  • 应用语言偏好
  • 通知设置

2. 第三方服务

InnerVital 不使用任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。

3. 儿童隐私

InnerVital 面向 13 岁及以上用户。我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。

4. 数据删除

如需删除所有数据，卸载应用即可。设备上所有本地存储的数据将被一并清除。

5. 政策更新

我们可能会不定期更新本政策，最新版本将始终发布在本页面。
